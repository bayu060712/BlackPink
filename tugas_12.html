About BLACKPINK

The Revolution

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa.

Debuting in August 2016 with their single album "Square One", which featured "Whistle" and "Boombayah", BLACKPINK quickly became one of the most popular K-pop groups worldwide.

They are the highest-charting female Korean act on the Billboard Hot 100, peaking at number 13 with "Ice Cream" (with Selena Gomez), and on the Billboard 200, peaking at number two with "The Album".

Record Breaking

  • First female K-pop group to perform at Coachella
  • Most-viewed music video by a K-pop group on YouTube ("Ddu-Du Ddu-Du")
  • First K-pop girl group to have two albums sell over 1 million copies
  • Most-subscribed music artist on YouTube
  • First Korean female group to receive a certification from the RIAA
